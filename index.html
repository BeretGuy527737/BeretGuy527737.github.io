Respectfully, why does this site look like it's from the 90s?

Thank you for asking tactfully! In the literal sense, it looks like it's from a few decades ago because I created it using technologies from a few decades ago. Most websites created today use some kind of website building or at least hosting service, of which I am doing neither. This website is currently hosted on my desktop and is comprised entirely of html and css files I wrote. As for why I chose to do this, 1. it's cheaper (so far my only cost for this site has been the $11 domain name), 2. it's a learning experience (I do enjoy programming, despite the descration of the field currently taking place at the hands of AI), and 3. I believe that in an era where every website is expected to have smooth, animated UI, an appealing layout and color scheme, and probably an AI chatbot in the bottom right corner, it's refreshing to have a website that possesses nothing to distract from the content itself.
